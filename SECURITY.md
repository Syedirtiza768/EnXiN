# Security Policy

The EnXi team and community take security issues seriously. To report a security issue, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Syedirtiza768/EnXiN/issues).

You can help us make EnXi and all its users more secure by following responsible disclosure practices.

We appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose your findings. We'll endeavor to respond quickly, and will keep you updated throughout the process.
